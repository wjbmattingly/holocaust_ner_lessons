A: Well, we...you see Poland was divided...half Po land and half Germany. We were...I mean half Russia, half Germany. We...I was born on the German side; but we wound up on the Russian side. And we had it very bad, because we were a big family. We had no food, nothing. So we tried to get back home, which what belonged to the German side. At that time, the Russians took us. There was a lot of people like me. They took us all; and they packed us in trains, you know--not passenger trains--and they took us to Siberia. Thousands of people, you know. And they just start to wake everybody; and said, "Get up! Get up! Get up!" And they just took us in trucks. And they took us to the trains, and we were closed in. And you boiled the water. So...in order it should be a lot. I mean, it was like 20 people; and everybody shoul d get a spoonful. So it got thick.
